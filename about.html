About Mager SACCOS LTD

Mager Saving and Credit Cooperative Society (SACCOS) Ltd. was established on June 30, 2024 (Sene 23, 2016 E.C.). In our inaugural year, we successfully mobilized 7,185,912.52 ETB in savings from 348 members.

Our Vision

To be a trusted national financial institution making a tangible contribution to citizens' social and economic lives.

Our Mission

To enable citizens to develop a saving culture and transform their lives through accessible credit services.

Our Core Values

  • Integrity
  • Fairness
  • Transparency
  • Accountability
  • Inclusivity
  • Social Responsibility
