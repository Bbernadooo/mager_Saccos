Our Financial Services

Mager SACCOS LTD provides a wide range of savings and credit options designed to empower our members and foster a culture of financial growth.

Saving Options

We offer diverse saving plans with competitive interest rates starting from 8%.

  • Regular Saving: Standard saving plan for all members.
  • Interest-Free Saving: Savings compliant with non-interest preferences.
  • Voluntary (Interest) Saving: Flexible options based on member needs.
  • Fixed-Time Saving: Deposits for a specific, predetermined duration.
  • Children’s Saving: Building a financial foundation for minors.
  • Housing Saving: Targeted for future home-related goals.
  • Vehicle Saving: Targeted for automobile purchases.
